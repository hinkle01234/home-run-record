Baseball has been played for over 100 years! Barry Lamar Bonds was born on July 24, 1964, in Riverside, California, to baseball player Bobby Bonds. He made history by setting the single-season and career records for home runs. Hank Aaron was born February 5,1934, in Mobile, Alabama. He was the third of eight children. Baseball player Babe Ruth was born on February 6, 1895, in Baltimore, Maryland. Over the course of his career, Ruth went on to break baseball's most important slugging records, including most years leading a league in home runs, most total bases in a season, and highest slugging percentage for a season. Born on May 6, 1931, in Westfield, Alabama, Willie Mays began his professional baseball career in the Negro Leagues before joining the New York Giants in 1951. Celebrated for his superb all-around play, he was twice named MVP and finished among the all-time leaders in home runs and hits.
